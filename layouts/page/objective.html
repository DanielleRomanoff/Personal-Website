ABOUT ME

Education

I started my post secondary education right after high school. My first Bachelor of Science degree is in Psychology. It was a great experience going through college right after high school and I learned a great deal both in and out of the classroom.

When I returned to school for my second degree, I had three small children at home. When my sons and I speak of those two and one half years now, they have clear memories of mom studying hard and doing lots of homework. I earned a Bachelor of Science degree in Mathematics with a focus in education. Somehow, even with three small children at home, I managed to graduate cum laude. Again, I learned a great deal. However, this time, I also had the chance to teach at the same time.

Many times as parents we forget to model what we tell our children we expect from them. I thankfully had this opportunity while earning my math degree. Not every one gets the chance to demonstrate good study habits to their children and make such an impression on them at a young age. I was fortunate to have that opportunity and since they remember even now, it has clearly made an impact.

The time to go back for an MS in Software Engineering came a few years ago. I wasn't going to let it go by and so I returned once more to college. I earned an MS in Software Engineering, Summa Cum Laude, from Stevens Institute of Technology. With two sons still in high school, it again gave me the opportunity to teach by example.

Last but certainly not least has been my education through Lambda School. The focus on data science and machine learning has been challenging but exciting. The power of computers is amazing. The skills I've acquired and the opportunity Lambda School has provided is a gift and I look forward to using these skills in a new role.
